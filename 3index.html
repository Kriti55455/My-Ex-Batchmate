As you stood by

In the K-drama As You Stood By, childhood friends Jo Eun-su and Jo Hui-su plot to murder Hui-su's violently abusive husband, Noh Jin-pyo. Driven by trauma and desperation, the women stage his disappearance with the help of a laborer who looks like him.

The plan falls apart when the laborer returns to extort them, leading the women into a complex web of lies and criminal investigations. Ultimately, they confess to their actions and serve time in prison. After their release, they rebuild their lives in Vietnam, supported by Jin So-baek

Strangers from Hell

Yoon Jong-woo moves from the country to Seoul for a new job. He rents a cheap room at Eden Studio, a creepy dorm. He soon realizes his neighbors are a gang of psychotic killers who torture and murder people. The leader, a dentist, manipulates Jong-woo into embracing his own dark side.

Devil Judge

he Devil Judge is a dystopian legal drama set in a chaotic, virus-ravaged version of the country where citizens harbor deep resentment toward corrupt leadership.It follows a ruthless, enigmatic chief judge who turns his courtroom into a televised reality show, allowing the public to vote on verdicts and dish out severe punishments to corrupt elites.

Highschool Frenemy

The High School Frenemy plot centers on the forced merger of two rival school campuses, Udon Phithak and Thep Burapha. When hot-headed transfer student Shin reunites with his former best friend-turned-enemy, Saint, their unresolved trauma and bitter fallout ignite a volatile classroom war that forces the school to change.

Hannibal

Hannibal is a psychological thriller that follows the complex relationship between FBI special investigator Will Graham and the brilliant psychiatrist Dr. Hannibal Lecter. Graham, who has a unique ability to empathize with serial killers, seeks Lecter's help to catch other murderers, unaware of Lecter's own dark and murderous tendencies

Silence of the Lambs

Silence of the Lambs is a psychological horror film that follows FBI trainee Clarice Starling as she seeks the help of the imprisoned cannibalistic serial killer Dr. Hannibal Lecter to catch another serial killer, Buffalo Bill. Lecter provides insight into Buffalo Bill's psyche, while Clarice navigates her own fears and traumas in a tense and suspenseful investigation.

Psychopath Diary

Psychopath Diary is a dark comedy thriller that follows the story of a timid office worker Yook Dong-sik who accidentally witness a murder and finds a serial killer's personal diary. Shortly after, he gets into an accident and gets amnesia. Thinking the diary is his own, he believes he is a brutal killer. He uses his fake "psychopath" mindset to stand up to his bullies
